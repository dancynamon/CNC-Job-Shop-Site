Capabilities

Our Services

Two core disciplines backed by modern equipment, experienced operators, and rigorous quality control.

01

CNC Machining

Precision milling and turning for prototypes through short-run production. We work in metals, plastics, and composites with tolerances as tight as ±0.005".

Milling

  • 3-axis & 5-axis CNC milling
  • Envelope up to 40" × 20" × 20"
  • Surface finish down to 16 Ra µin
  • Complex 3D contours & pocketing

Turning

  • CNC lathe with live tooling
  • Up to 12" diameter × 24" length
  • Threading, grooving, knurling
  • Bar-fed for production runs

Materials

  • Aluminum (6061, 7075, 2024)
  • Stainless Steel (303, 304, 316)
  • Titanium, Brass, Copper
  • Delrin, HDPE, Nylon, PEEK

Finishing

  • Anodizing (Type II & III)
  • Powder coating
  • Bead blasting & tumbling
  • Passivation & electropolish
02

Digital Printing

Large-format UV, solvent, and latex printing on rigid and flexible substrates. From one-off prototypes to high-volume signage runs.

UV Flatbed

  • Direct-to-substrate printing
  • Bed size up to 4' × 8'
  • Rigid substrates up to 2" thick
  • CMYK + White + Clear coat

Roll-to-Roll

  • Up to 64" wide output
  • Vinyl, banner, fabric, film
  • Solvent & latex inks
  • Lamination & mounting

Substrates

  • Dibond / ACM panels
  • Acrylic, polycarbonate, PVC
  • Coroplast, foam board, wood
  • Adhesive vinyl, mesh, canvas

Applications

  • Exterior & interior signage
  • Vehicle & fleet wraps
  • Trade show displays
  • Architectural panels & wayfinding
How It Works

Our process.

1

Submit

Send CAD files, drawings, or design files with specs. STEP, IGES, DXF, PDF, AI, and most common formats accepted.

2

Review

We review for manufacturability, confirm materials and finishes, and send a detailed quote — typically within 24 hours.

3

Produce

We program, machine or print, inspect, and finish your parts. QC documentation available on request.

4

Deliver

Parts are carefully packaged and shipped or available for local pickup. Lead times typically 1–3 weeks.
